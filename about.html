About Technology Aid LTD

Founded in 2019, Technology Aid Ltd. has quickly established itself as a premier provider of tailored business solutions and innovative digital services. Based in the heart of the tech industry, our mission is to deliver unparalleled service quality and efficiency, helping our clients to thrive in a competitive marketplace.

At Technology Aid Ltd., we believe in a holistic approach to service delivery. Our method involves a thorough understanding of our clients' needs, enabling us to provide bespoke solutions that are both innovative and effective. From Property Preservation to Software Development, each project is approached with a commitment to quality and client satisfaction.Our diverse range of services includes Web Design & Development, Vendor Management, and Business Process Outsourcing (BPO), among others. Each service is executed by a team of experts who are leaders in their respective fields, ensuring cutting-edge solutions and impeccable execution.At Technology Aid Ltd., our commitment extends beyond delivering excellent services. We are dedicated to contributing to the economic growth of Bangladesh by empowering our employees and enhancing their skills through continuous professional development. This dedication not only boosts local economies but also enriches the professional lives of our team members.We invite you to explore the opportunities and solutions offered by Technology Aid Ltd. Partner with us and benefit from a collaboration that values quality, efficiency, and transformative outcomes. Let's work together to create a future that is prosperous and successful.

Our CEO & Managing Director

MD MAMUN MIAH

Founder & CEO

Technology Aid is a next-generation global technology company that helps enterprises reimagine their businesses for the digital age. We've innovated for four decades to build our technology products and services, guided by a world-renowned management philosophy. Our culture thrives on invention and risk-taking, all while maintaining a relentless focus on customer relationships. Technology Aid also takes pride in its many diversity, social responsibility, sustainability, and education initiatives. Enterprises across industries stand at an inflection point today. In order to thrive in the digital age, technologies such as analytics, cloud, IoT, and automation occupy center stage. In order to offer enterprises the maximum benefit of these technologies to further their business objectives, Technology Aid offers an integrated portfolio of products and services through three business units. These are IT and Business Services (ITBS), Engineering and R&D Services (ERS), and Products and Platforms (P&P).
